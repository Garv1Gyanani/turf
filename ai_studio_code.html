The Science of Suspension

Why Choose Suspension Over Traditional Mat Yoga?

By utilizing high-strength silk hammocks, aerial yoga partially or fully supports your body weight. This reduces spinal compression, deepens joint mobility, and allows for supported alignment changes impossible on the floor.

Improves Flexibility

Deepen hamstring and pelvic stretches gently with safe posture variations.

Builds Core Strength

Suspension naturally forces deep stabilizing core activation across every sequence.

Reduces Stress

Gentle inversion practices trigger natural hormonal responses that soothe your nervous system.

Beginner Friendly

Every pose can be scaled. No background in gymnast or traditional yoga required.
